# Reliability Signals

This section explains how data reliability signals are generated.

In this system, anomaly detection starts from signal design,
not from machine learning models.

---

## Scope

- Validation signals
- Drift detection
- Structural anomaly detection
- Signal aggregation

---

## Key Topics

- Completeness / Missing Detection  
- Duplicate Detection  
- Drift Detection  
- Structural Anomaly  

---

## Role in System

Reliability signals are the foundation of the entire system.

They are used as inputs for risk calculation,
root cause analysis, and ML features.
